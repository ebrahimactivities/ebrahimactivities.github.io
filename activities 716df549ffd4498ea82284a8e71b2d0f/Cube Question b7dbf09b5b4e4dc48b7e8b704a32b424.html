A small note ⇒ the way I approached this problem was to look at each face on it’s own, so it’s not politically correct to write the surface integral (integral with circle through it) as you’d normally see in 158. That’s because it’s actually a closed surface integral, whereas the integral sign with the S on the bottom (see above) is for non-closed surfaces. Since a face of a cube is a non-closed surface, I’ll use the latter.
